This is a list of live action LGBT characters in television and radio. This list is for bisexual, gay, lesbian, and transgender fictional characters in various television (includes TV movies and web series), radio, and podcast programs. The orientation can be portrayed on-screen, described in the dialogue or mentioned. It is organized by orientation and alphabetically by surname (i.e. last name) or singular name if there is none.

Roles include lead, main, recurring, supporting, and guest.
